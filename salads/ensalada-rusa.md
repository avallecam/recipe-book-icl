# Ensalada Rusa (Peruvian Potato and Vegetable Salad)


## Ingredients

- **4** medium potatoes, peeled and diced
- **2** carrots, peeled and diced
- **1 cup** peas (fresh or frozen)
- **1/2 cup** green beans, chopped (optional)
- **1/2 cup** mayonnaise
- **1 teaspoon** yellow mustard (optional)
- **Salt and black pepper** to taste
- **1 tablespoon** lime or lemon juice (optional)
- **Fresh parsley** chopped, for garnish
- **Lettuce leaves** for serving (optional)


## Instructions

1. **Cook the Vegetables:**
   - Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Add the diced potatoes and
     carrots and cook for about 8–10 minutes until tender but still firm.
     Add the peas (and green beans, if using) during the last 2–3 minutes of
     cooking. Drain and let cool completely.

2. **Prepare the Dressing:**
   - In a large bowl, mix the mayonnaise with mustard (if using), lime or lemon
     juice, salt, and black pepper.

3. **Combine the Salad:**
   - Add the cooled vegetables to the bowl with the dressing. Gently mix until
     all ingredients are evenly coated, being careful not to mash the potatoes.

4. **Adjust Seasoning:**
   - Taste and adjust salt, pepper, or lemon juice as needed.

5. **Chill:**
   - Cover and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes before serving to allow the
     flavors to meld.

6. **Serve:**
   - Serve the ensalada rusa chilled, garnished with chopped parsley and over
     lettuce leaves if desired.
